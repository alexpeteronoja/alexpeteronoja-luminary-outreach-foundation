An in-house seminar on leadership

Leadership is the cornerstone of success in any organization, community, or endeavor. It is the driving force that inspires teams, fosters innovation, and achieves meaningful change. Recognizing the importance of cultivating effective leadership, our NGO is thrilled to announce an upcoming in-house seminar titled "How to Harness the Quality of a Good Leader." This event promises to be an enlightening and transformative experience for all attendees, offering valuable insights and practical strategies to unlock the potential of great leadership.

Scheduled to take place on the 26th – 27th August 2024, this seminar will bring together passionate individuals, team members, and community stakeholders who are eager to enhance their leadership skills. The event will feature a lineup of distinguished speakers, each bringing their unique expertise and perspectives to the table.

Meet Our Esteemed Speakers

Eric Miracle – Speaker I

Eric Miracle will delve into the core qualities that define a good leader. Her session will focus on self-awareness, emotional intelligence, and the ability to inspire and motivate others. Attendees can expect to walk away with a deeper understanding of how to lead with authenticity and purpose.

Emmanuel Williams – Speaker II

Emmanuel Williams will explore the practical aspects of leadership, including decision-making, conflict resolution, and team-building. His session will emphasize the importance of adaptability and resilience in navigating challenges and driving success.

El-Nathan Kenneth – Guest Speaker

El-Nathan Kenneth will share inspiring stories and lessons from his journey. His talk will highlight the role of vision, integrity, and innovation in shaping impactful leaders. Attendees will gain valuable insights into how to lead with confidence and create a lasting legacy.

What to Expect from the Seminar

The seminar is designed to be interactive, engaging, and highly informative. Participants will have the opportunity to:

  • Learn from real-life examples and case studies.
  • Engage in thought-provoking discussions and group activities.
  • Network with like-minded individuals and build meaningful connections.
  • Gain practical tools and techniques to apply in their personal and professional lives.

Why Attend?

Leadership is not just about holding a title; it’s about making a difference. Whether you’re a seasoned leader or aspiring to take on a leadership role, this seminar will equip you with the skills and knowledge to excel. By attending, you will:

  • Enhance your ability to lead with confidence and empathy.
  • Discover strategies to overcome common leadership challenges.
  • Gain inspiration to drive positive change in your organization and community.

Join Us!

We invite all members of our NGO and the wider community to join us for this transformative event. The seminar will take place on the 26th – 27th August, 2024 at 9PM at on Google Meet https://bit.ly/LOFleadership. Don’t miss this opportunity to learn from some of the best minds in leadership and take your skills to the next level.

Leadership is not just about leading others; it’s about leading yourself. Be part of this inspiring journey and unlock the leader within you!
