About The Organization

Our History

The Luminary outreach foundation began as a movement by the end of 2023 , it was later duly incorporated in July 2024 . our mandate is clear ,which is to help the children and young persons of the unserved population in our communities.

what is imperative to LOF is reaching out to the child in need , disabled persons, mentally impaired persons to strive to the fullness of their potential.

The LOF have overtime strive through economic difficulties to meet our ultimate goal of promoting national excellence, this is the reflection of our DNA wherever we go .

The voices and face of those we reached out to and those we are yet to,helps us to be recommitted into protecting the rights of the underserved , therefore we are always open for innovation and partnership.

Our Foundation

The Luminary outreach foundation began as a movement by the end of 2023 , it was later duly incorporated in July 2024 . our mandate is clear ,which is to help the children and young persons of the unserved population in our communities. what is imperative to LOF is reaching out to the child in need , disabled persons, mentally impaired persons to strive to the fullness of their potential . The LOF have overtime strive through economic difficulties to meet our ultimate goal of promoting national excellence, this is the reflection of our DNA wherever we go . The voices and face of those we reached out to and those we are yet to,helps us to be recommitted into protecting the rights of the underserved , therefore we are always open for innovation and partnership.

At Luminary Outreach, we are guided by a steadfast commitment to ethics that shape our interactions and decision-making processes.

Our Core Value

  • Confidentiality and Privacy: We safeguard sensitive information and maintain the highest levels of discretion.
  • Reliability and Equitability: We strive to be dependable, fair, and unbiased in all our endeavors.
  • Open Communication: We foster robust, interactive channels of communication to ensure seamless collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Our Vision

Luminary Outreach Foundation will consolidate ideas and efforts of great minds in ensuring we have a better society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create awareness to the society that will bring tremendous change to the state of affairs in the state which includes academic policies, rural area interventions and symposiums across various fields in order to secure an equitable future.

Our Ethics

  • Maintaining High level of Confidentiality and Privacy.
  • Being Reliable and Equitable.
  • Maintaining interactive and robust channels to communication.

Our Aims and Objectives

  • Provide access to quality educational resources and activities for underprivileged youth and children.
  • Provide agricultural materials to farmers to boost productivity.
  • Provide life skills workshops to prepare youths for the job market.
  • Raise awareness about issues affecting youths and children with disabilities and advocate for policy changes.
  • Promote physical and mental health through various programs and services.
