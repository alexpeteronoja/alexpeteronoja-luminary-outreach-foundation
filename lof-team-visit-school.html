Spreading Light Through Education: LOF Visits Command Day Secondary School

At Luminary Outreach Foundation, we believe that education is the light that dispels the darkness of ignorance. As part of our mission to empower communities and support underprivileged children, our team recently conducted a school outreach program aimed at fostering hope, learning, and growth.

On 11th of April, 2025, we visited Command Day Secondary school in Kaduna State, where we had the opportunity to interact with students, teachers, and members of the local community. It was a day filled with smiles, laughter, and heartfelt moments as we distributed educational materials including exercise books, pens, pencils, and other learning resources.

Our goal was simple but impactful: to provide tools that aid in learning and inspire these children to pursue their dreams. For many of the students, even a single notebook or pen can make a world of difference in their academic journey. The joy on their faces reminded us why we do what we do.

Beyond distribution, our volunteers also spent time engaging with the students, encouraging them to stay focused, dream big, and believe in their potential. We believe that no child should be limited by circumstance, and every child deserves the opportunity to succeed.

This outreach is just one of many steps in our ongoing commitment to educational development and youth empowerment. We are grateful to all our donors, partners, and volunteers who made this possible.

Together, we are lighting up the future one child at a time.
